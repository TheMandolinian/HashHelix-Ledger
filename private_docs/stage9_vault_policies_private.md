# Stage 9 — Vault Tier Policies

Institutions must declare how they store HashHelix data:

## HOT Tier
- Online
- Immediate query
- 30–90 day retention

## WARM Tier
- Medium-term
- Low-frequency queries
- Archived operations

## COLD Tier
- Permanent archive
- Immutable
- Decommissioned workloads

The vault policy is included in every anchor envelope.
